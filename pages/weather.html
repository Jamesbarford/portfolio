FCC Weather App

Brief

Exercise from Free Code Camp, the goal was to create a local weather app following this user story:

  • I can see the weather in my current location.
  • I can see a different icon or background image (e.g. snowy mountain, hot desert) depending on the weather.
  • I can push a button to toggle between Fahrenheit and Celsius.

(Please ensure your location is enabled!)

See the Pen Weather App by James Barford-Evans (@JamesBE) on CodePen.
